Important: Affiliate Relationship Disclosure

Our website contains affiliate links. This means if you click on a link to a service provider and make a purchase, we may earn a commission at no additional cost to you.

Awin Affiliate Program

We participate in the Awin affiliate program, which uses tracking cookies to monitor when you click an affiliate link and make a purchase. This tracking allows us to receive commissions for qualifying purchases.

How Affiliate Tracking Works

  • When you click on an affiliate link, a tracking cookie is placed on your device
  • This cookie tracks your visit to the service provider's website
  • If you make a purchase, the tracking system attributes the sale to our affiliate link
  • We receive a commission for qualifying purchases
  • Important: Affiliate tracking does not affect the price you pay
